Roadmap

Jun. 2017 The project research begins;
Dec. 2017 Release the CoAlpha program and establish the community;
Jan. 2018 Launch the development of digital currency index investment strategy.
Mar. 2018 Start the private fund sale.
Apr. 2018 Release the digital currency index investment strategy.
May. 2018 Finish setup of the blockchain R&D team; Reach tentative collaboration agreement with mainstream cryptocurrency exchanges.
August. 2018 Complete the asset management protocol products MVP based on Ethereum.
Nov. 2018 Complete the asset management protocol products based on Ethereum and provide corresponding smart contracts from the funds end to the trading end. The platform 1.0 goes online, and the investment and community functions are available.
Dec. 2018 Operate the investment community, enrich the investment product system on the platform, and provide diversified investment products, such as index, quantitative arbitrage products, and index enhancement strategies etc.
In 2019 Q2 Migrate to better base blockchain. Help 3rd party developers to develop more applications for the ecosystem. Research own base blockchain solution based on feedback from all parties in the ecosystem.
In 2019 Q4 Blockchain goes online for application. The platform develops to be the first global fund product platform of digital currencies.
